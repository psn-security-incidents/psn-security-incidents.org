#Why This Site Exists

Online gaming account thefts are a widespread and under-documented problem. Affected users frequently report difficulties navigating support channels, unclear resolution processes, and in some cases account bans triggered by actions taken while the account was under unauthorised control.

This site collects anonymous, user-submitted incident reports to document reported experiences, identify patterns in how attacks occur, and provide actionable guidance for affected users. No personal information is ever published.
